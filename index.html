Carolyn's painting

MEDIUM: OIL PAINT
Girl in flower field.
This painting is inspired by black artist Kehinde Willey. The processs of painting was extensive. I first printed out my reference picture. Then I sent my image to the projector to traced the outline using a projector. I incorportated some of his trademark style using patterns for the sky above the mountains. The subject I referenced for my painting is a picture of my mom when she was young.
